Base Configuration Region Support

In 8.0, Guidewire provides support for the following core regions in the base configuration:
•
United States (English)
•
Great Britain (English)
•
Australia (English)
•
Canada (English)
•
Canada (French)
•
France (French)
•
Germany (German)
•
Japan (Japanese)
Regional formats control how BillingCenter formats dates, times, numbers, monetary amounts, and the names of people and companies.
