Invoice Assembler Plugin Is Called During Charge Invoicing Process

During the charge invoicing process for a new charge, the Invoice Assembler plugin is called near the end of the operation. In BillingCenter versions 8.0.0‑8.0.3, the charge invoicing process did not call the Invoice Assembler plugin.
To identify that the plugin was called during the charge invoicing process, the plugin method’s context parameter is set to TC_BILLINGINSTRUCTION.
