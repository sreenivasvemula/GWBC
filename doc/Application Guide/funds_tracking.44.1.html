Account Funds Tracking

Customer service representatives often have difficulty in making sense of the flow of funds through the system when they need to respond quickly to requests from insureds. The challenge is in using transactional data to understand how BillingCenter has aggregated, directed, and redirected money for various uses. Funds tracking simplifies and consolidates transactional data, thus providing a clear overview of the flow of money. Customer service representatives can view the history of where funds came from and how they were used.
