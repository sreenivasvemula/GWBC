Producer Write-offs

Producer write-offs can occur for a variety of reasons. For example, a receivable is under collection, so the carrier decides not to pay commission to the producer. In this case, the carrier writes off the producer's commission reserve. A less common example occurs when a carrier decides not to collect commission that the producer owes because of a return premium. In this case, the carrier does a commission recovery write-off to clear negative commission reserves.
