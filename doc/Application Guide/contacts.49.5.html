Searching for Contact Information

Use the Search tab to see a list of all contacts associated with accounts, policies, or producers. To search for a particular contact, perform either of the following methods.
•
From the Search → Contacts → Search Contacts screen, select the Accounts, Policies, or Producers tab. Click the Search button to see a list of contacts for the relevant entity.
•
From the Search → Contacts → Search Contacts screen, enter values in the Search Criteria area, such as the Name or City. Click the Search button. Then click the tab of the entity whose contacts you want to view.
