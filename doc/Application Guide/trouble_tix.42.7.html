Trouble Ticket Object Model

The TroubleTicket has an array of join entities (TroubleTicketJoinEntities) that provide a relationship between the trouble ticket and accounts, policies, policy periods, and producers. The Hold entity has a one-to-one relationship (foreign key) to the TroubleTicket entity. So a trouble ticket hold is always tied to an existing trouble ticket and cannot exist on its own. Each Hold is associated with accounts, policies, policy periods, and producers through the TroubleTicketJoinEntity. The Hold entity has an array of HoldType objects. HoldType is an entity that indicates what kind of hold (such as an InvoiceSending hold or a Disbursements hold) and a date that specifies when to release the hold.
