Changing the Sidebar Width

You can resize the sidebar to made it wider or narrower than it currently is. This change remains in effect until you clear the layout preferences.
To change the width of the sidebar
1.
Position the mouse pointer over the right border of the sidebar. The pointer turns into a double arrowhead .
2.
Drag the sidebar border to the new width.
