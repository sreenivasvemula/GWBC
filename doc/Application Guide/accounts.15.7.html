Closing an Account

To close an account click Close Account on the account summary screen.
BillingCenter does the following:
1.
Checks whether the account can be closed. An account can be closed if the following conditions are met:
a.
The account is not already closed.
b.
All of the policy periods on the account are closed.
c.
The remaining balance on the account is zero.
2.
Sets the close date of the account to the current system time. The base product displays the account close date on the account Summary screen, but does not use it.
