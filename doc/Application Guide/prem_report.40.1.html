BillingCenter Application Guide : Additional Features of BillingCenter : Premium Reporting

Premium Reporting

Premium reporting is a billing process where insureds submit periodic reports to specify their current, up-to-date exposure. The reports are sent to BillingCenter, typically from an external Policy Administration System (PAS). Using the up-to-date information, BillingCenter can bill the insured for their actual rather than the estimated exposure.
Premium reporting is available for policies that use direct bill or agency bill processing. The premium reporting operation is nearly identical for both billing processes. Differences between them are described in the text.
