Invoice Item Detail Screen

BillingCenter tracks the events that happen to every invoice. To access the Invoice Item Detail screen for any invoice item, go to the Charges screen for an account or policy. Then click the Event Date link in the Invoice Items detail view for the item of interest.
In the previous graphic, the numbers point out the actions in BillingCenter that caused a series of item events to occur. The table below explains the details.
