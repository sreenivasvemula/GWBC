Trouble Tickets and Holds

This topic describes how to use trouble tickets and holds to manage and resolve customer issues. Trouble tickets are part of the exception handling process in BillingCenter and can be opened at the account, policy, or producer level. They are intended to track the process used to resolve issues customers raise about their accounts. For example, a customer calls her insurance company’s billing center to dispute a charge on her bill. An investigation reveals that the charge in question was performed on the wrong account. The trouble ticket is connected to both accounts (the one that was incorrectly charged and the one that was supposed to be charged) to monitor and ultimately resolve the issue. Holds block automated system behavior while billing problems are investigated and corrected.
