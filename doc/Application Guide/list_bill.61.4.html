Account-level Charges for List Bill Accounts

BillingCenter always uses a monthly stream for all account-level charges.
If the list bill payer incurs an account-level charge, such as a late fee or a payment reversal fee, BillingCenter first looks for an existing monthly invoice stream. If no monthly stream exists, BillingCenter creates it. The account-level charges are placed on the monthly invoice stream. However, the new monthly stream is used for account-level charges only. This is the only case where BillingCenter ever creates an invoice stream dynamically for a list bill account.
If there is more than one monthly invoice stream, the InvoiceStream plugin is called to determine which invoice stream to use. For more detail, see “Invoice Stream Plugin” in the Integration Guide.
Note: A list bill payer—especially one that has a long established relationship with the carrier—rarely incurs account-level charges.
