My Agency Items Screen

The My Agency Items screen is divided into five areas from which you can view details on specific activities associated with producers that:
•
Have you as an account representative.
•
Have at least one exception or unapplied or suspense payment.
Activities shown include payment and promise exceptions; unapplied payments; late payments and promises; and suspense payment items.

Accessing My Agency Items

To Access My Agency Items
Navigate to Desktop → My Agency Items.
To View the List of Exceptions or Other Items
Choose one of the five tabs at the top of the screen.
To View Details About Any of the Items that are Listed
Click the producer’s name.
