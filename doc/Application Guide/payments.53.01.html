BillingCenter Application Guide : Direct Bill Processing : Payment and Distribution

Payment and Distribution

A payment is a sum of money sent to a carrier to apply to a BillingCenter account. A distribution describes how money is applied towards a set of invoice items. This topic discusses how BillingCenter records payments and then distributes the funds to pay for an account’s unpaid or partially paid invoice items.

BillingCenter also makes payments – such as disbursements (refunds), commissions, and regulatory fees and taxes. Disbursements and commissions are discussed in other sections of this guide. See “Disbursements” for details on disbursement handling and “Commission Plans” for details on commission payments. Regulatory fees and taxes are recorded in BillingCenter, but the funds are passed through to the appropriate regulatory agencies.
