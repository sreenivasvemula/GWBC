Working with Billing Plans

In general, a billing plan determines:
•
Invoice lead time and non responsive lead times (due dates).
•
Invoice fees, such as those for insufficient funds (NSF).
•
Whether to suppress an invoice for a low balance.
•
What level of invoice item detail to show (if and how invoice items are to be combined).
•
How to handle disbursements and payment requests.

Associating Billing Plans with Accounts

A billing plan is associated with an account at the time that the account is created. When a Policy Administration System sends a request to create the new account, the request must include the billing plan. You also must specify a billing plan when you create a new account manually.
