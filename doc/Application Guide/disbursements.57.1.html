BillingCenter Application Guide : Direct Bill Processing : Disbursements

Disbursements

This topic describes how BillingCenter handles disbursements. This includes how disbursements are created, approved, or rejected. A disbursement is a refund that pays a customer back funds from an account. In BillingCenter, a Disbursement entity is created that has a trigger date and is assigned a dollar amount and a due date.
This topic includes: 
•
Manual Disbursements
•
Automatic Disbursements
•
Disbursing Suspense Payments
•
Disbursement Approval or Rejection
